All data processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or transmitted over the internet.

What We Do

  • Process your CSV files locally in your web browser
  • Validate and clean UK data formats (phone numbers, NI numbers, postcodes, sort codes)
  • Generate cleaned data files for download
  • Provide validation reports and error analysis

What We Don't Do

  • Store your data on our servers
  • Transmit your files over the internet
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your usage or collect personal information
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes

GDPR Compliance

This application is designed to be fully GDPR compliant:

  • Data Minimization: We only process the data you explicitly choose to clean
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is only used for the stated purpose of validation and cleaning
  • Storage Limitation: No data is stored beyond your current session
  • Transparency: All processing happens locally and is visible to you
  • User Control: You have complete control over your data at all times

Technical Details

The application uses modern web technologies to ensure your data never leaves your device:

  • File processing happens entirely in JavaScript within your browser
  • No server-side processing or data transmission
  • All validation rules and algorithms run locally
  • Downloaded files are generated client-side

Browser Storage

We may use minimal browser storage for:

  • Remembering your phone format preference (International vs UK)
  • Storing temporary processing state during your session

This data is automatically cleared when you close your browser and contains no personal information.

Third-Party Services

This application uses:

  • Tailwind CSS CDN: For styling (no data collection)
  • Your Web Browser: For all processing and file operations

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the main application.
